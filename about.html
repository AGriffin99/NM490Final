About

My name is Anitra Griffin. My project is about women in technology and making this information more accessible to girls pursuing technology. The structure of my project will be webpages of interactive and informative sub sections, where there is opportunity to learn about different women of color in the technology field and possibly some activity to see if a person wants to go into the field. The project will be documented and presented on a local website. It will look like an interface to learn new basic technology skills and learn about a woman of color in the field.
